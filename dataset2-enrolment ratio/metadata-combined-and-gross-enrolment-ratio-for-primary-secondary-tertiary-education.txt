Description:

    of age expressed as a percentage of population in the theoretical age group for
    that level of education'

  - '- From 2005, the gross enrolment ratios include private education institutions.'
  - '- From 2010, the gross enrolment ratios are adjusted for residents who are based
    overseas and cannot be enrolled in Singapore.'
  - '- Year 1 Diploma courses are classified as post-secondary non-tertiary under
    ISCED 2011 from 2016 onwards.'
